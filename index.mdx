---
title: "Introduction"
description: "Deal Brain is a System of Action where reps do the day to day work of running a great deal."
---

Built on the abundant context that is already created from calls, emails, debriefs, etc. it listens, comprehends, and helps reps act with that context. Actions take context and Deal Brain organizes that information for the moments when it is useful, all while keeping the system of record in sync aiming to keep data real-time, all-the-time.
